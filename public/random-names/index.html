List Randomizer

This form allows you to pick a random number of items of a List or arrange all the items of in random order.

Step 1: Enter List Items

Enter your items in the field below, each on a separate line. Items and can be numbers, names, email addresses, etc. Duplicate Items will be removed. A maximum of 20,000 characters are allowed.


Step 2: Enter your group selection

Enter the number of random items to pick from the list. To random the whole list type 0.


Step 3: Go!
